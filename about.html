About Me

Hello! I’m Thisal Yapa, an undergraduate student in Computer Engineering at the University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka. I’m deeply passionate about programming, computer systems, and electronics — always eager to learn how things work from the ground up.

My interests lie in areas such as systems programming, embedded design, and low-level graphics. I enjoy building small but meaningful projects that help me explore how software and hardware interact, and how elegant code can bring ideas to life.

Beyond academics, I love experimenting with new technologies, developing personal projects, and continuously learning to expand my technical and creative skills.

Skills & Interests

  • Core Areas: Systems Programming, Embedded Systems, Computer Graphics
  • Tools & Platforms: Linux, Git, Make, VS Code
